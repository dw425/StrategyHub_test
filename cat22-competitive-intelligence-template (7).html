SWOT Analysis Framework

Internal strengths/weaknesses vs. external opportunities/threats

💪 STRENGTHS Internal • Helpful
What do we do well? What unique resources do we have?
🏆 Core Competencies
  • Industry-leading AI/ML capabilities
  • Fastest time-to-value (avg 6 weeks)
  • Deep vertical expertise (Financial Services, Healthcare)
💰 Financial
  • Strong cash position ($50M runway)
  • 120% net revenue retention
  • Profitable unit economics (LTV:CAC 4:1)
👥 People & Culture
  • Top-tier engineering talent (40% ex-FAANG)
  • Customer-obsessed culture (NPS 72)
  • Low turnover (12% annually)
⚠️ WEAKNESSES Internal • Harmful
Where can we improve? What are we lacking?
📉 Market Position
  • Limited brand awareness outside core markets
  • Smaller partner ecosystem than leaders
  • No presence in APAC region
🔧 Product Gaps
  • Mobile app functionality limited
  • Integration catalog smaller than competitors
  • Reporting/analytics needs improvement
🏢 Operational
  • Support response times lag industry (4hr vs 1hr)
  • Documentation gaps in advanced features
  • Single-threaded on key accounts
🚀 OPPORTUNITIES External • Helpful
What market trends can we leverage?
📈 Market Trends
  • AI adoption accelerating (TAM growing 25% YoY)
  • Digital transformation budgets increasing
  • Shift from on-prem to cloud (60% → 85% by 2026)
🎯 Strategic
  • Competitor X struggling post-acquisition
  • Mid-market segment underserved
  • Partnership opportunity with Salesforce
🌍 Expansion
  • EMEA market entry (€2B addressable)
  • Adjacent vertical expansion (Manufacturing)
  • Platform/marketplace opportunity
⛈️ THREATS External • Harmful
What obstacles do we face?
⚔️ Competitive
  • Microsoft bundling with O365 (free tier)
  • Well-funded startups entering space
  • Price war initiated by Competitor Y
📜 Regulatory
  • GDPR compliance costs increasing
  • AI regulation uncertainty (EU AI Act)
  • Data localization requirements
💸 Economic
  • Recession risk → budget cuts
  • Talent cost inflation (20% YoY)
  • Cloud infrastructure costs rising

TOWS Strategic Options Matrix

S-O Strategies
Leverage strengths to capture opportunities
  • Use AI leadership to capture digital transformation budgets
  • Expand to EMEA using strong unit economics
  • Partner with Salesforce leveraging integration expertise
W-O Strategies
Address weaknesses to capture opportunities
  • Build partner program to expand ecosystem
  • Invest in mobile to capture growing segment
  • Target mid-market where brand matters less
S-T Strategies
Use strengths to minimize threats
  • Compete on value/TCO vs Microsoft free tier
  • Use NRR to weather economic downturn
  • AI expertise for regulatory compliance features
W-T Strategies
Minimize weaknesses and avoid threats
  • Improve support before recession scrutiny
  • Build integrations to reduce switching risk
  • Document AI features for regulatory readiness
