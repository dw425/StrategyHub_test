Agentic AI represents the next frontier for financial services, with autonomous systems capable of executing trades, adjusting risk exposure, and personalizing customer interactions without human intervention. Early adopters are deploying AI agents for customer service, loan underwriting, and regulatory reporting, achieving 40-60% efficiency gains while improving accuracy and compliance.
