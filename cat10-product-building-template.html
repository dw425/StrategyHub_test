Sustainable Methodologies

Frameworks for long-term product success

üîÑ
Iterative Agile

Short cycles with continuous feedback. Ship small, learn fast, adapt constantly. Prioritize working software over documentation.

  • 2-week sprints with clear goals
  • Daily standups, weekly retros
  • Continuous deployment to staging
  • Feature flags for safe releases
üìä
Lean Product

Validate before building. Minimize waste by testing assumptions early. Build-Measure-Learn at maximum speed.

  • Hypothesis-driven development
  • MVP for every major feature
  • Kill features that don't perform
  • Metrics define success, not opinions
üîÅ
Continuous Everything

Automate the entire pipeline. From commit to production with confidence. Quality is built-in, not bolted-on.

  • CI/CD for every change
  • Automated testing at all levels
  • Infrastructure as code
  • Observability from day one
üèóÔ∏è
Platform Thinking

Build foundations, not just features. Enable teams to move fast independently. Invest in developer experience.

  • Internal platforms as products
  • Self-service capabilities
  • Golden paths for common patterns
  • Reduce cognitive load on teams
