SRE Concepts & Reliability

Service Level Indicators, Objectives, and Error Budgets

The Four Golden Signals (Google SRE)

⏱️
Latency
Time to serve a request. Track both successful and failed request latencies.
❌
Errors
Rate of failed requests. Include explicit (500s) and implicit (wrong data) errors.
📊
Traffic
Demand on your system. HTTP requests/sec, transactions/sec, or retrievals/sec.
💾
Saturation
How "full" your system is. CPU, memory, disk, network utilization.

Alerting Strategy

From detection to resolution — effective alert management

👤
RESPOND <15 min MTTA
1 Acknowledge alert
2 Join incident channel
3 Review runbook
4 Begin investigation
5 Update status page
✅
RESOLVE <60 min MTTR
1 Identify root cause
2 Apply fix / rollback
3 Verify resolution
4 Close incident
5 Schedule postmortem
P1
Critical
Service down, revenue impact. Page immediately.
Response: <5 min
P2
High
Degraded service, some users affected.
Response: <30 min
P3
Medium
Non-critical issue, workaround exists.
Response: <4 hours
P4
Low
Minor issue, no immediate impact.
Response: Next business day
